Triggers

The microinteraction will be triggered when the mouse hovers over the text.

Rules

Once the microinteraction is triggered by a mouse hover, the text italicizes and an line slides in horizontally from the left to the right under the text. When the user hovers off the text, the italicized text fades back to normal and the underline slides back out from the right and exits the left side.

Feedback

When the user hovers, they will see the line slide in underneath the text and the text italicize. When they hover off, the flow reverses - the line slides back out and the italicized text fades back to normal.

Loops and Modes

No loops or modes.
